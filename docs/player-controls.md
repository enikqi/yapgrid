# Player Controls

The YapGrid player includes common playback and viewing controls.

## Playback

- Play and pause.
- Seek bar for jumping to a specific point.
- Rewind and forward controls.
- Playback speed selection.

## Audio

- Volume control.
- Mute and unmute.

## Streaming Options

- Server X/Y/Z selector.
- Quality selector when multiple qualities are available.
- Buffering and loading indicators while content is preparing or switching.

## Subtitles

- Subtitle selector.
- Local subtitle upload for `.srt` and `.vtt` files.
- Subtitle translation when available.
- External subtitle support through embed parameters.

## Viewing

- Fullscreen control.
- Picture-in-picture support when allowed by the browser.
- Responsive layout for desktop, tablet, and mobile screens.

## Mobile Behavior

On mobile devices, controls may appear in a compact layout. Some browser-level behaviors, such as autoplay and fullscreen, depend on the device, browser, and user settings.
